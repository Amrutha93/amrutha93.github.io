About Us

The Book Club is made up of readers just like you. There’s nothing we love more than finding a book that we can’t wait to share with others. If it’s a thriller, it had better make our hearts pound. If it’s a story about friends and family, we want to feel the tears pricking our eyes and hear the laughter coming out of an office down the hall. If it’s a cookbook, it had better inspire one of us to bring home-baked cookies in the next day, and if it’s non-fiction, it had better be chat-worthy enough to see us clustering around the coffee maker at eight o’clock in the morning. You know what we mean, and it’s what we call a “really really” book. The sort of book that really makes you feel something strong.

And we would really love to have you as part of our community. See you on the site!
